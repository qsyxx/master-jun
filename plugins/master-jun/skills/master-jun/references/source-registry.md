# 在线原典来源登记

Use these as discovery and verification starting points. Check edition metadata and scan quality before quoting.

## Preferred repositories

- Chinese Text Project: searchable classical text, data wiki, and scanned-library links. OCR pages can contain errors; consult linked images.
- Wikimedia Commons/Wikisource: public-domain scans and transcriptions with page-level provenance when available.
- University, national library, museum, and archive scans: prefer these for edition verification.

## Quotation protocol

1. Locate the passage in a searchable transcription.
2. Check the corresponding scan or a second edition.
3. Record title,卷/篇, edition when known, and URL.
4. Quote briefly; paraphrase the surrounding argument.
5. Mark uncertain characters or disputed attribution.
